OUR SERVICES

We offer a wide range of complete wedding planning services to deliver a one of a kind wedding, whether you are planning an intimate gathering or a big fat Indian wedding !!!

ENTERTAINMENT

We provide a range of options relating to entertainment at the wedding and related functions, including live acts, live music, DJs, etc.

FOOD AND CATERING

Food is special. Yes, and it excites us to think and provide the best catering services, with high quality food served in an elegent a way.

BRIDAL MAKE-UP

We consult and coordinate with professional stylists for hair, makeup, mehendi, accessories, etc as per your vision and budget.

Photography

We arrange wedding photography specialists for your wedding-related event ( pre-wedding shoot, engagement shoot and bridal photo shoot, etc ).

VENUE & LOCATION

We are experts on providing wedding, event venues, and location visits based on the client’s theme, budget and requirement.

HOSPITALITY

DECOR AND DESIGN
